ParaCo, a Paracord Company

Background

ParaCo is a paracord company that started fall 2015 as a fundraiser for CMA, a small school in Tulsa. This very small company consisted of a group of four people, but with their efforts they managed to raise over $500. Now most of them are still in contact but the group has sort of split apart as they all went to different schools and only one stayed.

They became Each part of there own company, some of them had skyrocketing business, some their business failing after they left. Finally, one downgraded his paracord business to a side job, and is learning to become a coder, and that coder is me. I am indeed a lost member of this company, losing business as I go, and in need of help to keep going.
